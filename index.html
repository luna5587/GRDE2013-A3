Clean Up
Australia Day

Every year on the first Sunday of march, Australians unite to conserve the environment and end waste. Clean Up Australia Day challenges all Aussies to pop on some gloves, grab a rubbish bag and come together to clean up their community.

What's the big issue?

One of Australia’s biggest crises - waste.
Australians are creating more waste than ever, so we need to reduce the rubbish that enters our environment and the waste that goes to landfill. With rampant consumerism, unnecessary waste and poor recycling habits, Australians are making too much rubbish - and not doing enough about it.
